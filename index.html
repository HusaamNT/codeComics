About

The purpose of this website is to connect Marvel fanatics with one another while learning about their favourite characters.

You are able to interact with other fan posts on either the Marvel Reddit page or search your favourite heros (or villains!) to get sent to the characters individual subreddit to explore their posts.

The future of this website is to be able to socialise with other users on this page by user logins, create a lore page to futher establish this as the go-to Marvel social page!
